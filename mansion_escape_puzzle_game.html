Welcome to Mansion Escape Puzzle Game ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information. 2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

Mansion Escape Puzzle Game is designed to be played without requiring you to create an account or provide personal information. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address directly.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our app, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Game Progress Data: Your current stage, completed puzzles, hint usage, and game statistics are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage to save your progress.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information, and device performance data.
  • Usage Analytics: Stage completion rates, time spent on puzzles, hint usage frequency, ad interaction data, and app crash reports to improve game performance.
  • Advertising Data: Information collected by our advertising partners (Google AdMob) including ad impressions, clicks, and advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android).
Important: All game progress data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or sync your game progress to any cloud servers. If you uninstall the app, your progress will be lost.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Game Functionality: To save your progress, track completed stages, and provide hints when requested.
  • App Improvement: To analyze user behavior, identify bugs, improve puzzle design, and enhance overall game experience.
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob and measure ad performance.
  • Performance Monitoring: To monitor app crashes, errors, and performance issues to maintain app stability.
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with the game and optimize puzzle difficulty and hint systems.

6. Data Storage and Security

6.1 Local Storage

Your game progress data is stored locally on your device using React Native's AsyncStorage. This data includes:

  • Current stage number
  • Completed puzzles list
  • Hint usage statistics
  • Game preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers. If you uninstall the app or clear app data, this information will be permanently deleted.

6.2 Security Measures

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. We implement reasonable security measures including:

  • Secure coding practices
  • Regular security updates
  • Encrypted data transmission when communicating with third-party services
  • Limited data collection practices

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

7. Advertising and Monetization

Mansion Escape Puzzle Game is free to play and is supported by advertising. We display the following types of ads:

  • Banner Ads: Displayed during gameplay at strategic intervals.
  • Interstitial Ads: Full-screen ads shown every 2 stages after puzzle completion.
  • Rewarded Video Ads: Optional ads you can watch to receive hints. Every 3rd rewarded ad reveals the puzzle answer.

These advertisements are served by Google AdMob and may be personalized based on your advertising identifier and usage patterns. You can control ad personalization through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
