Mansion Escape Puzzle Game – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Mansion Escape Puzzle Game (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We do not require account registration and do not directly collect personal information such as names or email addresses. However, third-party services integrated into the app may automatically collect certain data.

Automatically Collected Data

  • Device or Other IDs: Advertising ID (AAID/IDFA)
  • Approximate Location: Derived from IP address
  • App Activity: Gameplay interactions, ad views, ad clicks
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, language
  • Performance Data: Crash logs and diagnostics
Note: Game progress is stored locally on your device. Advertising and analytics data may be processed by third-party services.

3. How We Use Data

  • To display advertisements
  • Advertising and marketing purposes
  • Analytics and app improvement
  • Measuring ad performance
  • Fraud prevention and security

4. Advertising & Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may use mediation partners to display advertisements.

These services may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • IP address (approximate location)
  • App interaction and ad engagement data

Third-Party Privacy Policies

  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy

5. User Consent (GDPR / CCPA)

Where required by law, we request user consent for personalized advertising. You may change ad personalization settings through your device.

  • EU/EEA: GDPR consent supported
  • California (US): CCPA “Do Not Sell or Share” rights supported

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared with advertising and analytics partners as described in this policy.

7. Data Retention

  • Local game data remains until app removal
  • Advertising data is retained by third-party partners per their policies

8. Data Deletion

You may delete local data by uninstalling the app.

For data collected by third-party services, you may request deletion through:

  • Google Ad Settings
  • Your Google Account privacy controls
  • Contacting us at the email below

9. Children’s Privacy

This app is not primarily directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. International Users

Data may be processed in countries outside your own, including the United States.
