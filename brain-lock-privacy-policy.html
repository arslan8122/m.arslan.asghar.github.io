Welcome to Brain Lock: Number Puzzle ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our mobile application (the "App"). 2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Our App stores the following information locally on your device using secure local storage:

  • Game Progress: Your quiz progress, current round, and saved game state
  • High Scores: Your best scores and performance records
  • User Statistics: Total quizzes completed, correct answers, achievements unlocked
  • Brain Certificates: Certificates you've earned for completing puzzles
  • Settings Preferences: Your app settings including vibration preferences
  • Achievements: Your unlocked achievements and progress
  • Last Played Information: Timestamp of your last game session

Important: All this information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or third parties. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the App.

2.2 Device Information

We may automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • Device type (iOS or Android)
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Device identifiers (for ad serving purposes)

2.3 Usage Information

We may collect information about how you interact with the App, such as:

  • Features you use
  • Time spent in the App
  • Game sessions and quiz completions

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Improve Our Services: To deliver the App's functionality, maintain your game progress, and improve user experience
  • To Personalize Your Experience: To remember your preferences and settings
  • To Display Advertisements: To show relevant advertisements through Google AdMob (see Section 5 for more details)
  • To Analyze App Performance: To understand how users interact with the App and identify areas for improvement
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: To meet legal requirements and respond to legal processes

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdMob

Our App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use information about your device and app usage to show you personalized ads. This includes:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • Location data (if permitted)
  • App usage data
  • Interests and demographics (inferred from your activity)

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our App, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's advertising settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

4.2 Local Storage Services

We use AsyncStorage (React Native) to store your game data locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not shared with third parties.

4.3 Camera Roll Access

Our App may request access to your device's photo library to save Brain Certificates you've earned. This permission is optional and only used when you choose to save a certificate to your gallery. We do not access, view, or transmit any other photos from your device.

5. Advertising

Our App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. We show the following types of ads:

  • Interstitial Ads: Full-screen ads shown after completing quizzes or after multiple wrong answers
  • Rewarded Ads: Optional video ads you can watch to receive hints or skip difficult questions
  • Native Ads: Banner-style ads integrated into the App's interface

We comply with Google's AdMob policies and request your consent before showing personalized ads (where required by law, such as in the EU under GDPR).

Note: You can control ad personalization through your device settings as described in Section 4.1.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
