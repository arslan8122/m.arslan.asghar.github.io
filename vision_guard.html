Privacy Policy for VisionGuard: 20-20-20 Timer

Last Updated: December 31, 2025

Introduction

Arslan Asghar ("we," "our," or "us") operates the VisionGuard: 20-20-20 Timer mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Information Collection and Use

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

VisionGuard stores all user data locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Data stored locally includes:

  • Focus session history (timestamps, durations, focus modes used)
  • User statistics (total focus time, session counts, streak data)
  • Badge progress and unlocked achievements
  • Level and experience points (XP)
  • App settings and preferences (theme, notification settings)
  • Ad-free pass status and premium feature unlocks

This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing app data

Third-Party Services

VisionGuard integrates the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the App. AdMob may collect and process certain data to provide personalized ads and measure ad performance.

Information AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Device information (manufacturer, model, OS version)
  • App usage data
  • Location data (if location permissions are granted)

AdMob Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Opt-out of personalized advertising:
You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking

Notifee (Notifications)

We use Notifee to deliver local notifications for focus sessions and break reminders. Notifee operates entirely on your device and does not collect or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Permissions

VisionGuard may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send focus session reminders and break alerts
  • Vibration: To provide haptic feedback for notifications
  • Run at Startup: To maintain timer functionality and notifications
  • Prevent Phone from Sleeping: To keep the timer running during active sessions

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and do not involve data collection or transmission.

Children's Privacy

VisionGuard does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The App is designed for general audiences and does not require users to provide any personal information.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Using device lock screens (PIN, pattern, fingerprint, face ID)
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Installing apps only from official app stores

Analytics and Crash Reporting

VisionGuard does not currently use any analytics or crash reporting services. No usage data is collected or transmitted to external servers.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the App's Analytics and Settings screens
  • Deletion: You can delete all app data by uninstalling the App or clearing app data through your device settings
  • Portability: Your data is stored locally and remains with you on your device

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy
  • Providing an in-app notification for significant changes

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services (such as educational articles or external resources). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any information.

Data Retention

Your data is retained locally on your device for as long as the App is installed. Data is automatically deleted when you:

  • Uninstall the App
  • Clear the App's data through device settings
  • Perform a factory reset on your device

International Users

VisionGuard is available worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device, no data is transferred internationally. However, third-party services (like AdMob) may process data according to their own privacy policies.
