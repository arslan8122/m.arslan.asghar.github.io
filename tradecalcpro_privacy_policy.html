Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

TradeCalc Pro ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the TradeCalc Pro mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

TradeCalc Pro is designed to work fully offline. We do not collect, transmit, or store personal data on external servers.

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Saved projects and calculations
  • User preferences (such as theme settings)
  • Notes entered by the user

All stored data remains on your device and is never shared with us or any third party.

2. Personal Information

We do not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or location.

3. Permissions

TradeCalc Pro may request limited device permissions solely to provide core functionality, such as:

  • File access (to export or share PDFs)
  • Local storage access (to save projects and calculations)

These permissions are used only within the app and never for data collection or tracking.

4. Third-Party Services

TradeCalc Pro does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are in full control of your information. Removing the app will permanently delete all locally stored data.

6. Children’s Privacy

TradeCalc Pro is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
