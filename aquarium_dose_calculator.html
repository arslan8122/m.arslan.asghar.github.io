Privacy Policy for AquaDose Pro

Last Updated: December 27, 2025

AquaDose Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Important: By using AquaDose Pro, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using the App:

  • Aquarium Data: Tank volumes, dosing calculations, product information, and custom notes you enter
  • Calculation History: Records of your saved calculations and dosing schedules
  • Custom Products: Information about aquarium products you add to your personal database
  • Preferences: App settings and notification preferences

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our App, we and our third-party partners may automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent in the App, interaction with advertisements
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, app crashes, and performance data
  • Advertising ID: Your device's advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID on Android, IDFA on iOS)

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may collect information including:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device and network information
  • App interaction data
  • Location data (approximate, based on IP address)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 App Functionality

  • Perform dosing calculations and provide accurate results
  • Save your calculation history locally on your device
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Send dosing reminders and notifications (if you enable this feature)
  • Provide access to premium features unlocked through rewarded ads

2.2 Advertising

  • Display personalized advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Measure ad performance and engagement
  • Limit ad frequency to improve user experience

2.3 App Improvement

  • Analyze app usage to improve features and user experience
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user behavior

2.4 Legal Compliance

  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to legal requests
  • Protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage, including:

  • Calculation history
  • Saved aquarium configurations
  • Custom products
  • App preferences
  • Premium feature unlock status

3.2 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption during transmission
  • Secure storage practices
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal information

Note: While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

4.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use cookies and similar technologies to provide personalized ads based on your interests.

AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

How Google uses data: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

You can control personalized advertising:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking
  • In-App: Watch a rewarded ad to remove banner ads for 24 hours

AquaDose Pro is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information from our systems.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the App's services:

  • Calculation History: Stored locally until you delete it or uninstall the App
  • Usage Data: Retained for analytical purposes for up to 24 months
  • Ad Data: Managed by Google AdMob according to their retention policies

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

7.1 Access and Control

  • View Data: Access your calculation history and saved data within the App
  • Delete Data: Clear individual calculations or your entire history from the History screen
  • Export Data: Export your calculation history (premium feature)
  • Modify Data: Edit or update your saved calculations and preferences

7.2 App Deletion

Uninstalling the App will delete all locally stored data from your device. To permanently delete all data:

  1. Clear all history within the App (Settings → Clear All Data)
  2. Uninstall the App from your device
  3. Contact us if you want us to delete any data we may have collected

7.3 Opt-Out Rights

  • Notifications: Disable dosing reminders in App Settings
  • Personalized Ads: Use device settings to limit ad tracking
